Social Feeds

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Social Feeds ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Social Feeds widget platform, accessible at social-feeds-app.netlify.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Social Feeds:

  • Account information: Phone number or Google account details used to sign in.
  • Instagram data: When you connect your Instagram account, we store your Instagram username and a long-lived access token (valid for 60 days) to display your Instagram feed.
  • Google Business data: When you add a Google Reviews widget, we store your Google Place ID to retrieve and display your business reviews.
  • Usage data: Basic logs for debugging and service improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To display your Instagram feed and Google Reviews on websites where you embed our widgets.
  • To generate embed codes for use on third-party websites.
  • To improve the reliability and performance of our service.

3. Instagram Data

We access the following Instagram data on your behalf:

  • Profile information (username, profile picture, follower count)
  • Media posts (images, videos, captions, timestamps)

This data is used solely to render your Instagram feed widget on websites you authorize. We do not sell, share, or use your Instagram data for advertising or any purpose beyond displaying your feed.

You can revoke our access to your Instagram account at any time by visiting Instagram's App Permissions settings and removing Social Feeds.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase, a cloud database provider with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. Access tokens are stored encrypted and are only used server-side to fetch your feed data.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Service providers: Supabase (database), Netlify (hosting), and Twilio (SMS verification) as necessary to operate the service.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

6. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • Instagram / Meta: Subject to Instagram's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Places API: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Revoke Instagram access at any time via Instagram's settings
  • Delete your Social Feeds account and all associated data

8. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, email us at the address below. We will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days. You may also disconnect your Instagram account directly from Instagram's app permissions settings, which will invalidate your stored access token.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact Us
