Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Social Feeds ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Social Feeds widget platform at social-feeds-app.netlify.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Phone number or Google account details used to sign in.
  • Instagram data: When you connect your Instagram account, we store your username and a long-lived access token (valid 60 days) to display your feed.
  • Google Business data: When you add a Google Reviews widget, we store your Google Place ID to retrieve and display your reviews.
  • Usage data: Basic logs for debugging and service improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To display your Instagram feed and Google Reviews via embeddable widgets.
  • To generate embed codes for use on third-party websites.
  • To improve the reliability and performance of our service.

3. Instagram Data

We access the following Instagram data on your behalf:

  • Profile information (username, profile picture, follower count)
  • Media posts (images, videos, captions, timestamps)

This data is used solely to render your Instagram feed widget. We do not sell, share, or use your Instagram data for advertising or any purpose beyond displaying your feed.

You can revoke access at any time via Instagram's App Permissions settings.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase with encryption at rest and in transit. Access tokens are stored server-side only and never exposed to end users.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data, except with service providers necessary to operate the platform:

  • Supabase (database hosting)
  • Netlify (web hosting)
  • Twilio (SMS verification via Supabase)

6. Third-Party Services

  • Instagram / Meta: Subject to Instagram's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Places API: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Revoke Instagram access via Instagram's settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data

8. Data Deletion

To delete your account and data, email us at the address below. We will complete deletion within 30 days. You can also disconnect Instagram directly via Instagram's app permissions, which invalidates your stored token immediately.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time and will reflect changes by updating the date above.

Contact Us

Questions or data deletion requests: privacy@639hz.com
